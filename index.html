About Us

Welcome to Smartbiz XL Eats – your seamless solution for curated dining experiences at XLRI. Born out of a vision to enhance food accessibility and variety for students and staff, we aim to connect the XLRI community with top local eateries. With four popular restaurants now onboard, our platform delivers quality, convenience, and choice right to your campus life.

Amazon’s Smartbiz model
We focus on empowering small businesses while providing our users with a seamless, tailored ordering experience. Our goals include building strong relationships with local restaurant partners, ensuring quality and value in every meal, and delivering a fast, user-friendly interface that meets the unique needs of campus life. We are committed to using technology to streamline food ordering, bringing you delicious, diverse options without the hassle.
